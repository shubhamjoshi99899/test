ABOUT COMET

COMET Services is career development, counseling, mentoring, and training initiative that has been providing guidance to the children of Indian soldiers and other needy students from remote places who are deprived of career development related guidance, either due to geographical limitations or due to financial constraints. All schools and coaching centers in India run without considering the career development aspect in their plan. This major flaw in our education system prohibits the students to achieve as per their best abilities. COMET works towards resolving these known gaps by enabling students to identify their potential skills and guide them closely to convert their potential skills into success stories. .

The story of COMET Services began as a voluntary service in 1995, at Assam Rifles campus(Shillong). It started as an individual effort from Ram Datt Bhatt and gradually with increasing support from professional colleagues, it has now taken the shape of an ed-tech career counseling and training hub, helping 100s of needy students from various parts of the country like Uttarakhand, northeast Indian states, Delhi, and Hyderabad.!

Today, we are privileged to connect with 1000s of students, which allows us to transform the career of the needy students into a glorious success story.



How COMET Works

COMET helps the students in following ways –

  1. Providing career development related guidelines to the needy students.

  2. Providing career counselling sessions in schools that are not able to employ career counselling staff.

  3. COMET has provision for COMET-study-centre for class 12 pass out students and for fresh graduates who are struggling to make a good career.

  4. Helping with COMET study centre option for higher education



COMET Career Councelling Ladder

  1. Class 6 - know about some popular career options.

  2. Class 7 - know more about different career options.

  3. Class 8 - know about career paths by different streams.

  4. Class 8 – know about career paths by different subjects.

  5. Class 9 – self-assessmenow to identify skills and capabilities.

  6. Class 9 –resolve career related doubts, to take right career decision.

  7. Class 10 – execute the career plan.

  8. Class 11 – execute the career plan.

  9. Class 11 – Identify best universities & colleges based on careen choice

  10. Class 12 – execute the career plan.

  11. Class 12 – apply for different exams based on career choice



COMET – Career Counselling Services in Schools

With this service, we aims to collaboarate with schools that are not able to employ career counselling staff and help the to develop traditional schools into career oriented institutes that will be able to transform every student’s skills into success story.


COMET achieves this by identifying and and allocating counselling expert for these schools. Counselling expert performs following tasks –

  • Common group counselling at school level, including students from class 7 to class 12

  • Group counselling by classes, from class 7 to class 12

  • resolving career related doubts of students.
